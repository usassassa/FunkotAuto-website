Product Overview
----------
Funkot Auto DJ is a Windows desktop application specialized for Funkot — a high-BPM, DJ-centric dance music genre originating from Indonesia.

The software automatically mixes Funkot tracks with seamless transitions, reproducing the EQ and fader techniques of real Funkot DJs. The mixing behavior is modeled after the style of DJ SUMMER RANDCEL, an active Funkot DJ.

Key Features:
- 2-Deck Automated Mixing: Tracks are mixed automatically at user-set cue points, with real-time EQ visualization.
- Funkot Cue Editor (included, free to use): A precision waveform tool for setting Play Cue and Mix Out points. Supports 200x zoom, metronome sync, and independent offsets to handle Funkot's distinctive "downbeat" sections.
- LAN Streaming: Stream the DJ mix to other devices on the same network via QR code.
- WAV Recording: Automatically records the mix with a tracklist output.
- Audio Watermark (Streaming Premium only): Inserts customizable chime sounds at intervals to deter unauthorized recording of live DJ streams.
- Multi-language support: Japanese, English, and Bahasa Indonesia.

The app consists of two executables:
1. FunkotAutoDJ.exe — The main auto-mixing engine.
2. FunkyCueEditor.exe — The cue point editor (no restrictions, always free to use).
